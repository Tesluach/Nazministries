Biography

Where People Matter and grow in Christ together

Our Mission

To promote a holistic ministry that impacts and transforms the spiritual, physical and social condition of an individual which will yield a community of people equipped and empowered by the Gospel of Jesus Christ to change the world.

Our Vision

To be passionate disciple maker with practical Biblical teaching, a place of grace, where the Holy Spirit is transforming lives people.

Core Values

  • Christian
  • Holiness
  • Missional
  • Respect
  • Family

Objective

  • To be a mission outreach in training pastors and leaders.
  • Promoting peace and reconciliation in the community through love of Christ.
  • To helps orphans, widows and youth by providing opportunities for education, gender-based violence awareness, worsening of human trafficking and child protection.
  • To create awareness aimed at safe drinking water, proper sanitation and improvement of Hygiene behavior in society.
  • To create gender equality and the empowerment of women as effective ways to combat poverty to stimulate development that is truly sustainable.
  • To promote the youth talents through sports activities.
