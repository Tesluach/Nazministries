Welcome

Warm Christian greetings to you, and welcome to The Church of the Nazarene South Sudan in Uganda website! “Where People Matter and Grow in Christ Together.”

The South Sudan church of the Nazarene in Uganda was established in 2016 in Kampala with the small group members of Nazarenes as fellowship program before they get church where they can worship in and in the following year church planting expanded and established five branches and it’s main headquarter in Kampala namely; United church of the Nazarene (Nyanama), Seeta church of the Nazarene, Galeli church of the Nazarene, Bethlehem church of the Nazarene (both Bethlehem & Galilee are in refugees camp), Kigumba church of the Nazarene and Rhino church of the Nazarene (refugee camp) churches make a continuous progress in term of increasing their congregations year by year. The total weekly attendance members of six South Sudan churches of the Nazarene in Uganda are 1,185 peoples.

Community

Church of the Nazarene is Community of faithful believers, Fellowship together, exists to provide a family-friendly environment in which individuals are led to become authentic followers of Jesus Christ, disciple maker and equipped to fulfill God’s purpose in the world. We are Interconnected where everybody finds their place of service. We are a group of Christian people who are dedicated to helping you identity and take your next step in the safety and freedom of community of faith! Through Next Move, Small Groups/life group, and Outreach where you can connect with others as you grow in your relationship with Christ.
