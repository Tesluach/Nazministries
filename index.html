Welcome

Warm Christian greetings to you, and welcome to The Church of the Nazarene South Sudan in Uganda website! “Where People Matter and Grow in Christ Together.”

Church of the Nazarene, we are a Protestant, Evangelical in the Wesleyan-Holiness Tradition. Organized in 1908, we are a Great commission Church (Matthew 28:19-20), presented in 162 countries! Church of the Nazarene is the Christian church, as we know it today, came into being following the crucifixion and resurrection of Jesus Christ. This happened in the first half of the first century subsequent to the outpouring of the Holy Spirit on the day of Pentecost as reported in Acts 2. Within less than 300 years the Christian movement became the most powerful force in the Roman world. In the year 323, Christianity became the official religion of the Empire.

In the Middle Ages, several Christian reform movements arose. In the 16th century, the Protestant Reformation led by people like Martin Luther and John Calvin gave birth to many of the Christian denominations we know today. In the early 18th century, an Evangelical Revival swept across England. That revival was fueled by evangelist George Whitefield and the ministry of two brothers, John and Charles Wesley. The Wesleys emphasized the possibility of a victorious life through the indwelling presence of the Holy Spirit. The preaching, teaching, follow up work and social ministries of the Wesleys gave birth to the Methodist movement.

Nazarene is a place where doctrine of Holiness is teaches, where God is saving, healing and delivering souls from the bondage of sin. We are the model church that help each person, encounter with God, to believe in Jesu, grow deeper in Holy Spirit, belong to family, loving God and others.

In a day and age when the Word of God is being watered-down, it is good to know that there are a people making intentional discipleship, teaching life Transforming, changing hearts and lives through the empowerment of the Holy Spirit, and direction of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

Our Community

Church of the Nazarene is Community of faithful believers, Fellowship together, exists to provide a family-friendly environment in which individuals are led to become authentic followers of Jesus Christ, disciple maker and equipped to fulfill God’s purpose in the world. We are Interconnected where everybody finds their place of service. We are a group of Christian people who are dedicated to helping you identity and take your next step in the safety and freedom of community of faith! Through Next Move, Small Groups/life group, and Outreach where you can connect with others as you grow in your relationship with Christ.
